Lightning fast

WriterCMS was built with focus on speed. Readers should be able to enjoy your content as easily as possible, which is why we built WriterCMS with speed in mind. It is lightning-fast and a breeze to use, making it easy for you to publish your next post without having to wait for slow page loads.

developer_mode

Mobile first

By always thinking mobile-first, we managed to develop WriterCMS into a tool that works well on your smartphone. Since it is web based, no app is needed and it is supported by all major browsers. Just log in and start writing!

code

Customizable

All source code is released under the MIT license, enabling you to customize it as you wish. WriterCMS is built with the MEAN stack, following existing conventions and guidelines and will soon be fully documented.

What are you waiting for? Just start writing!
